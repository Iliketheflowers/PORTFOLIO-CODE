ABOUT THE FILM

This film is a student project and my first time directing.

It follows Hadryah, a drag queen based in Berlin, during a time of searching for belonging, for self-expression, and for a place that feels like home. Rather than telling a linear story, the film focuses on presence, atmosphere, and moments that feel intimate and real.

The film doesn't aim to define identity or give clear answers. Instead, it invites the viewer to witness a process of becoming, questioning, and existing in between.
