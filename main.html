Welcome to my Portfolio :D

I like observing things, noticing small details, and sometimes overthinking why they caught my attention in the first place.
This portfolio is basically the result of that.

I follow moods, curiosity, and moments that make me stop and look twice. If something feels interesting, awkward, or even slightly strange, that's usually a good. You'll find my photography, film, and art here. Sometimes it's about people, sometimes about vibes, sometimes just a feeling I couldn't shake off.

There isn't one big message, more like a collection of thoughts and moments I didn't want to keep only to myself.

Feel free to scroll and indulge. Or don't. FREE WILL!
Either way... thanks for being here!
